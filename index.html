Why Banana for Scale?

Everyone knows the size of the banana. No more arguing over meters or feet. Who knows what twelve inches looks like, anyway? By communicating in terms of banana, we can bridge the barrier or world-wide conversion units and use the only, true, universal unit. Whether describing weight or length, the eiffel tower or your living room tv, bananas are the common measurement we can all rely on.
